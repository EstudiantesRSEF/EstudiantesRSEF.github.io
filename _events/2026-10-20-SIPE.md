      <p style="text-align: justify;">The <strong>Spanish-Italian Physics Exchange (SIPE)</strong> is an annual event that brings together passionate physics students from Spain and Italy. Born from the desire to create a bridge between our countries, SIPE is more than just a conference; it's a unique scientific and cultural experience.</p>
                <p>Under our motto <em>"Fisica senza confini, Física sin fronteras"</em>, we aim to break down barriers, share innovative research, and build a lasting network of future physicists. Throughout the event, participants will enjoy a vibrant mix of academic talks, visits to cutting-edge laboratories, cultural tours, and unforgettable social events.</p>
