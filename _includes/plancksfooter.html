Grupo de Estudiantes RSEF

The Student Group (GdeE) of the Royal Spanish Society of Physics (RSEF) seeks to bring together all physics students in Spain, provide them with new opportunities at both national and international levels, and help improve the dissemination and teaching of physics. The GdeE is the National Committee Spain in the International Association of Physics Students (IAPS).
